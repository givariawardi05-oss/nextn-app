# **App Name**: BlackHorse Roastery Manager

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Provides a snapshot of key metrics including total sales, warehouse stock, roasted stock, and store stock to give an overview of the roastery's health.
- Purchase Invoice Management: Enables creation, tracking, and management of purchase invoices for green beans, updating the warehouse stock upon saving.
- Warehouse Inventory: Displays and manages the stock of green beans, providing insights into total stock, varieties, and average price, with filtering options for low stock.
- Roasting Batch Management: Allows for planning and tracking roasting batches, automatically deducting from warehouse stock and adding to roasted inventory.
- Inventory of Roasted Beans: Manages the stock of roasted beans, allowing transfer to the store inventory and providing details on total stock and value.
- Store Inventory: Tracks inventory in the store, enabling manual addition of stock and providing insights into total value and stock status.
- Transaction Monitoring: Keeps a log of all transactions including sales, purchases, and adjustments to give insights to income and expenses, including debit, credit, and running balance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#3F51B5) to convey professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, modern feel.
- Accent color: Bright cyan (#00BCD4) to highlight key actions and information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clear, readable text throughout the application.
- Use flat, consistent icons to represent different data types and actions. Icons should be simple and easily understandable.
- Implement a clean, card-based layout to organize information. Ensure ample spacing and clear hierarchy.
- Subtle transitions and animations should be used to provide feedback and enhance user experience, like loading spinners or confirmation messages.
